Our Founders

Enigma Elara

CEO

Enigma Elara, the enigmatic and somewhat eccentric CEO of Riddle Ready Services, is a mastermind in the art of perplexity. Rumored to have solved her first riddle before learning to walk, Elara has dedicated her life to spreading the joy of mental discomfort. With a wicked grin and an aura of mystery, she leads the company with a vision to make the world question everything – starting with the subscribers' sanity.

Puzzler Paul

Chief Riddle Officer

Puzzler Paul, our Chief Riddle Officer, is a walking database of diabolical puzzles and a connoisseur of perplexity. Armed with a smirk that can raise more questions than his riddles, Paul ensures that every personalized puzzle strikes fear into the hearts of subscribers. His motto: "If they're not scratching their heads, we're not doing our job."
